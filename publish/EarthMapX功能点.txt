 EarthMapX 应用功能总结
1. 丰富的地图图层资源
   覆盖基础地图、卫星影像、地形地貌、拓扑地图等多场景；
   基础图层：含BaseMap（多风格基础地图模板）、ArcgisTile（支持ArcGIS瓦片地图加载），满足通用浏览需求；
   专题图层：含Imagery（卫星影像）、Terrain（地形地貌）、Physical（自然地理）、Dark_Gray/Light_Gray（灰度风格），可精准呈现从宏观地球到微观地形的细节。

2. 三维地球与地形沉浸式可视化
   支持2D/3D视图无缝切换；
   三维模式可直观展示地球立体形态与地形起伏，支持自由旋转、缩放，实现沉浸式探索；
   可对地形进行夸张化渲染（如突出高山、深海），清晰呈现地貌特征，为地理研究、地质分析提供支撑。

3. 专业数据可视化与空间分析
   专题数据可视化：可加载并展示各类地理专题数据（如地震数据），支持按时间维度（全月、全天）呈现，通过红点直观显示地震位置与密度；
   空间测量工具：支持面积、长度、半径等几何量算，可绘制多边形、圆形等图形并自动计算参数，满足空间分析需求。

4. 多视图对比与图层精细化管理
   多视图对比：支持双视图同步展示，可同时加载不同图层或视角，直观对比地理信息差异（如不同地形渲染风格对比）；
   图层管理：每个图层可独立调整透明度、控制显示/隐藏状态，还能删除、排序图层，方便用户按需定制地图展示效果，打造个性化地理视图。

5. 广泛的应用场景
   适用于地理教育、科研分析、行业决策（如地质、环境、规划领域）；
   提供从“宏观地球认知”到“微观地理分析”的全流程地理信息可视化与探索能力，兼具专业性与易用性。


 EarthMapX Application Function Summary
1. Rich Map Layer Resources
    Covers multiple scenarios including base maps, satellite imagery, terrain landforms, and topological maps.
    Base layers: Include BaseMap (multistyle base map templates) and ArcgisTile (supports ArcGIS tile map loading) to meet general browsing needs.
    Thematic layers: Include Imagery (satellite imagery), Terrain (terrain landforms), Physical (physical geography), and Dark_Gray/Light_Gray (grayscale styles), enabling precise presentation of details from macro global patterns to micro terrain features.

2. 3D Earth and Terrain Immersive Visualization
    Supports seamless switching between 2D and 3D views.
    The 3D mode intuitively displays the Earth’s threedimensional shape and terrain undulations, supporting free rotation and zoom for immersive exploration.
    Allows exaggerated rendering of terrain (such as highlighting mountains and deep seas), clearly presenting landform characteristics to provide intuitive support for geographical research and geological analysis.

3. Professional Data Visualization and Spatial Analysis
    Thematic data visualization: Can load and display various types of geographical thematic data (e.g., earthquake data), supporting timedimensional (full month, full day) presentation and intuitively showing earthquake locations and density through red dots.
    Spatial measurement tools: Supports geometric calculations such as area, length, and radius, and can draw polygons, circles, and other graphics with automatic parameter calculation to meet spatial analysis needs.

4. MultiView Comparison and Refined Layer Management
    Multiview comparison: Supports simultaneous display of dual views, allowing loading of different layers or perspectives to intuitively compare geographical information differences (e.g., comparison of different terrain rendering styles).
    Layer management: Each layer can independently adjust transparency, control display/hide status, and support deletion and sorting, enabling users to customize map display effects on demand and create personalized geographical views.

5. Wide Range of Application Scenarios
    Suitable for geographical education, scientific research analysis, and industry decisionmaking (e.g., geology, environment, and planning fields).
    Provides fullprocess geographical information visualization and exploration capabilities from "macro Earth cognition" to "micro geographical analysis", combining professionalism and ease of use.
